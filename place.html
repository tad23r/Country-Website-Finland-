Interesting Places

Immerse yourself in the beauty and wonder of Finland's most interesting places.

Helsinki Cathedral

A neoclassical masterpiece with a distinctive green dome.

Discover Helsinki Cathedral

Rovaniemi

Rovaniemi, located on the Arctic Circle, is a place full of vibrant life and culture surrounded by pristine nature and breath-taking nature phenomena every day of the year.

Discover Rovaniemi

Turku Archipelago

On this journey, you will get to experience the beautiful Turku archipelago region on the west coast of Finland and the fresh flavours of the Baltic Sea.

Discover Turku

Tampere

Tampere is the sauna capital of the world – but there’s plenty more to it than that. Located in the Lakeland region, it’s on a scenic spot on a narrow isthmus between two great lakes.

Discover Tampere

Pihlajasaari, “Rowan island”

Pihlajasaari is the most popular island among Helsinkians to spend a sunny summer’s day. The island is famous for its picturesque sandy beaches and rocks, offering great spots for sunbathers and swimmers

Discover "Rowan Island"

Historical Places

Discover the hidden stories of Finland's rich history.

Old Porvoo

An easy and idyllic place to combine shopping and dining with an enchanting walk through history.

Discover Old Porvoo

Old Rauma

One of the few UNESCO World Heritage Sites in Finland, a stroll through Old Rauma is the perfect activity for any history-lover.

Discover Old Rauma

The National Library of Finland, Helsinki

The main building dates back to the 1840s, and the library is an integral part of the intact Neoclassical architecture of the Senate Square.

Discover Finland's Library

Senate Square

A historically significant area for Finnish architecture is the Senate Square.

Discover The Senate Square

National Museum of Finland

The National Museum of Finland, Kansallismuseo, offers visitors a captivating journey through the rich history of Finland

Discover Finland's Museum
